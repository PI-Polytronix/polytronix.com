About Polytronix, Inc.

Founded in 1983 in Richardson, Texas, Polytronix, Inc. began with a focus on specialized displays and glass, building a reputation for engineering excellence and product quality. Over the decades, we expanded our manufacturing capabilities to meet the evolving requirements of aerospace, defense, and industrial customers.

Today, Polytronix delivers engineered components and complete systems across displays, illuminated panels, bezels, cable assemblies, indicator lighting, box builds, machining and fabrication, and electronic assemblies. Our integrated approach ensures quality, consistency, and on‑time delivery across every program.

Our Divisions

Aerospace & Defense

Avionics displays, illuminated panels, cable harnesses, lighting, electro‑mechanical assemblies, box builds, and machining. Supported by certifications including AS9100D and ISO 9001:2015.

Industrials

Displays, harnesses, assemblies, and PCB manufacturing for medical devices, HVAC, energy, telecom, robotics, agriculture, and general industrial applications.

Smart Glass

Polyvision® switchable privacy glass and smart glazing solutions for architectural, healthcare, transportation, and specialty applications.
